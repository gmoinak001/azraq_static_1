What is Cryptocoin mining?

If you have already watched the video you must have got it by now that bitcoin is nothing but a ledger of encrypted information. Bitcoin mining or mining any currency refers to maintaining the ledger by renting out your computer system's processing power to the blockchain network. It requires thousands of graphics cards run in series to solve a math problem produced by the network and try and get the payout for that block. Clearly this requires a lot of investment from the miners side to arrange and setup the rigs. Apart from that the person needs to have a sound knowledge of entire system to be able to monitor it succesfully. These factors often make mining a tedious work.

What is Cloud mining?

Cloud mining is an alternative approach towards cryptocoin mining where people invest there share of money to build up the rig and get paid for whatever amount of mining done for their share of resources. It is less expensive than making your own setup. You also get a chance to earn more blocks per second as the entire system is working as one making money for everyone at the same time.
